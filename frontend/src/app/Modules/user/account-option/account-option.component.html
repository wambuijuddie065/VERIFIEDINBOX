ACCOUNT INFO > ACCOUNT OPTIONS

VerifiedProfile offers the following option to customize your account

Update your profile

Keeping your profile current helps us better serve your needs. Click here to update your profile.

Change your mail.

Do you want to change your email address? Click here to start the email changing process.

Change your password.

Do you want to change your password or make it more secure? Click here to start the password changing process.

Change your notification settings.

Select the type of communication you want to receive from us. Click here to start the selection process.

Change your time zone settings

Did you move? Click here to change your time zone.

Delete your account.

No longer need your account? Click here to start the account deletion process.

Manage users.

By setting up a Team Account, you will be able to invite multiple users to our email validation platform. Click here to start managing your account.

Manage contact list.

By setting up Contact Lists, you will be able to add a contact by adding their email addresses to the contact list. Contact lists can be used in Blacklist Monitoring tool when sending out alert emails. Click here to start managing your contact lists.

Password protected downloads

If you chose to disable this feature, all your downloads from Verified Profile will no longer be password protected. If your account is compromised, your data will be at risk. We strongly recommend keeping this option enabled. To disable this feature, you will receive an email with a link to confirm. Once you confirm, the feature will be disabled. You can re-enable it at anytime.

Two factor authentication

If you choose to enable this feature, your account will be more secure requiring you to use two-factor authentication when you log in.

Account assumption

If you choose to enable this feature, VerifiedProfile Tier 2 support team will have access to your account to assist you in troubleshooting any related account issues. Once enabled, it can be disabled at any time. It will be automatically be disabled in 7 days from activation.
