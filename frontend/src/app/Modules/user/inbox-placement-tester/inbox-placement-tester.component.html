Welcome To The Inbox Placement Tester.

The inbox placement tester is used to check if your emails/newsletters will make it to your recipient's inbox. This test will allow you to send an email to different mailbox providers around the world and quickly check to see the placement of that email.

Click to see the steps to perform an inbox placement test.

1. Enter a testing file name (this is to help you identify individual tests).

2. Choose which mailbox providers you wish to test by selecting from the dropdown.

3. Click "Create New Test".

4. Copy your custom test code from the box below, and insert into the footer of your email.

5. Copy the newly generated email addresses and add them to a new mailing list in your email marketing service.

Please note:Some mailbox providers will not deliver one-word emails, so be sure to test with a natural email or newsletter. Once you create your first test, the results will appear in the table below.
