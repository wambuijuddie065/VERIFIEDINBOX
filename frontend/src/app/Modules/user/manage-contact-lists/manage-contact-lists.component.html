Manage Contact Lists

Ensure Blacklist Monitoring alerts reach the proper users or individuals in your organization. Create a contact list below, and send invites to those relevant people.

Follow the steps below:

1. Create and name your contact list.

2. Once your list is created, click 'Manage Contacts'.

3. Click 'Invite a new contact' and enter the email address of the person you wish to add to the contact list.

A Freemium Plan or an active Tool Subscription Plan is required to manage contact lists.
