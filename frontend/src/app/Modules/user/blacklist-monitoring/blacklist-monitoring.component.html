Welcome To The Blacklist Monitoring.

Using our Blacklist Monitoring service will allow you to verify your status with over 200 different blacklists. And if your domain/IP is flagged you'll know which blacklisting company to contact about being delisted around the world and quickly check to see the placement of that email.

To ensure complete coverage, we recommend that you monitor both your domain and IP address. Unsure of what your domain IP address is? Use Google's free DNS tool here

Click to see the steps to begin monitoring a domain or IP.

1. Click to "Add New Monitor".

2. You will be prompted to enter a domain or IP to monitor, which contact list will receive the alerts, and if any notes are needed.

3. Once you are satisfied with the settings, click "Add" to create the monitoring alert.

For further information about our Blacklist Monitoring service and how to properly protect your domain/IP address, be sure to visit our documentation page.

Note: By default our alarm system will notify you when your domain or IP has been initially scanned, then, only if the status of your domain/IP has changed, i.e. it was added to a blacklist or delisted from one. The number of blacklists we check against will flactuate. To ensure proper monitoring, please make sure to monitor your domain, IPv4 and IPv6 addresses.
