Notification Settings

VerifiedInbox has the ability to send out email notifications to keep you up to date about different events and useful information. We understand that some notifications are important to some customers, but might not be of importance to you.

Account:

Notify me when my account is accessed by a new IP address.

Notify me about unused credits after 90 days of inactivity.

Email Validation and Scoring:

Notify me when my email is ready to be downloaded.

Notify me 7 days early before my uploaded files are deleted.

Notify me when my credit balance is under credits .

Deliverability Tools:

Notify me when my tests are completed.
